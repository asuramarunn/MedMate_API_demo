Disease: Influenza (Flu)
Symptoms: Fever, chills, sore throat, runny nose, cough, muscle aches

Disease: Migraine
Symptoms: Severe headache, nausea, vomiting, sensitivity to light, sensitivity to sound

Disease: Diabetes Mellitus
Symptoms: Excessive thirst, frequent urination, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision

Disease: Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)
Symptoms: Often no symptoms, headache, dizziness, shortness of breath, nosebleeds

Disease: Asthma
Symptoms: Wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, coughing, especially at night or early morning

Disease: Peptic Ulcer
Symptoms: Burning stomach pain, bloating, heartburn, nausea, intolerance to fatty foods

Disease: Pneumonia
Symptoms: Cough with phlegm, fever, chills, difficulty breathing, chest pain

Disease: Gastritis
Symptoms: Upper abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, bloating, indigestion

Disease: COVID-19
Symptoms: Fever, cough, loss of taste or smell, shortness of breath, fatigue

Disease: Chronic Kidney Disease
Symptoms: Fatigue, swelling in legs and ankles, decreased urine output, nausea, confusion

Disease: Tuberculosis
Symptoms: Persistent cough, weight loss, night sweats, fever, coughing up blood

Disease: Hepatitis B
Symptoms: Fatigue, jaundice, abdominal pain, dark urine, loss of appetite

Disease: Hepatitis C
Symptoms: Fatigue, joint pain, jaundice, nausea, loss of appetite

Disease: Dengue Fever
Symptoms: High fever, severe headache, pain behind the eyes, joint and muscle pain, rash

Disease: Malaria
Symptoms: Fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea

Disease: Typhoid Fever
Symptoms: Prolonged fever, abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation, headache, rash

Disease: Chickenpox
Symptoms: Itchy rash, fever, fatigue, loss of appetite, headache

Disease: Measles
Symptoms: High fever, cough, runny nose, red eyes, skin rash

Disease: Mumps
Symptoms: Swollen salivary glands, fever, headache, muscle aches, loss of appetite

Disease: Rubella
Symptoms: Mild fever, rash, joint pain, swollen lymph nodes, red eyes

Disease: Whooping Cough (Pertussis)
Symptoms: Severe coughing fits, whooping sound when breathing in, vomiting after coughing, exhaustion

Disease: Bronchitis
Symptoms: Persistent cough, mucus production, fatigue, shortness of breath, slight fever

Disease: Sinusitis
Symptoms: Facial pain, nasal congestion, thick nasal discharge, headache, fever

Disease: Otitis Media (Middle Ear Infection)
Symptoms: Ear pain, difficulty hearing, fluid drainage from ear, fever, irritability

Disease: Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye)
Symptoms: Red eyes, itching, tearing, discharge from eyes, swelling

Disease: Stroke
Symptoms: Sudden numbness or weakness, confusion, trouble speaking, vision problems, loss of balance

Disease: Heart Attack (Myocardial Infarction)
Symptoms: Chest pain, shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, lightheadedness

Disease: Heart Failure
Symptoms: Shortness of breath, swelling in legs, fatigue, rapid heartbeat, persistent cough

Disease: Arrhythmia
Symptoms: Irregular heartbeat, palpitations, dizziness, fainting, shortness of breath

Disease: Osteoarthritis
Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling, decreased range of motion, grinding sensation

Disease: Rheumatoid Arthritis
Symptoms: Joint pain, swelling, morning stiffness, fatigue, weight loss

Disease: Osteoporosis
Symptoms: Often no symptoms, bone fractures, loss of height, back pain

Disease: Gout
Symptoms: Sudden severe joint pain, swelling, redness, warmth, tenderness

Disease: Psoriasis
Symptoms: Red patches of skin, silvery scales, itching, dry skin, cracked skin

Disease: Eczema (Atopic Dermatitis)
Symptoms: Itchy skin, red patches, dryness, thickened skin, small bumps

Disease: Lupus
Symptoms: Fatigue, joint pain, skin rash, fever, photosensitivity

Disease: Anemia
Symptoms: Fatigue, weakness, pale skin, shortness of breath, dizziness

Disease: Hyperthyroidism
Symptoms: Weight loss, rapid heartbeat, irritability, heat intolerance, sweating

Disease: Hypothyroidism
Symptoms: Fatigue, weight gain, cold intolerance, dry skin, constipation

Disease: Celiac Disease
Symptoms: Diarrhea, bloating, weight loss, fatigue, anemia

Disease: Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
Symptoms: Abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, constipation, mucus in stool

Disease: Crohn’s Disease
Symptoms: Abdominal pain, diarrhea, weight loss, fatigue, fever

Disease: Ulcerative Colitis
Symptoms: Abdominal pain, diarrhea with blood, fatigue, weight loss, urgency to defecate

Disease: Gallstones
Symptoms: Abdominal pain after eating, nausea, vomiting, bloating, indigestion

Disease: Pancreatitis
Symptoms: Severe abdominal pain, nausea, vomiting, fever, rapid pulse

Disease: Epilepsy
Symptoms: Seizures, confusion, staring spells, loss of consciousness, uncontrolled movements

Disease: Parkinson’s Disease
Symptoms: Tremor, stiffness, slow movement, balance problems, difficulty speaking

Disease: Alzheimer’s Disease
Symptoms: Memory loss, confusion, difficulty completing tasks, mood changes, disorientation

Disease: Depression
Symptoms: Persistent sadness, loss of interest, changes in sleep, fatigue, feelings of worthlessness

Disease: Anxiety Disorder
Symptoms: Excessive worry, restlessness, muscle tension, difficulty concentrating, sleep problems
