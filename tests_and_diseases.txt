# List of medical tests and associated diseases

Complete Blood Count (CBC):
- Anemia
- Infection
- Leukemia

C-Reactive Protein (CRP):
- Inflammation
- Infection
- Autoimmune diseases

Blood Culture:
- Sepsis
- Bacterial infections

Chest X-ray:
- Pneumonia
- Tuberculosis
- Lung cancer

Sputum Gram Stain:
- Bacterial pneumonia
- Tuberculosis

ECG (Electrocardiogram):
- Acute myocardial infarction
- Arrhythmia
- Myocarditis

Liver Function Test (LFT):
- Hepatitis
- Cirrhosis
- Liver cancer

D-Dimer:
- Pulmonary embolism
- Deep vein thrombosis

Troponin I:
- Acute myocardial infarction
- Myocarditis

Urinalysis:
- Urinary tract infection
- Kidney disease
- Diabetes

---

# Diseases and their commonly used diagnostic tests

Community-acquired pneumonia:
- Complete Blood Count (CBC)
- C-Reactive Protein (CRP)
- Blood Culture
- Chest X-ray
- Sputum Gram Stain
- Urinalysis

Acute myocardial infarction:
- ECG
- Troponin I

Pulmonary embolism:
- D-Dimer
- Chest X-ray

Sepsis:
- Complete Blood Count (CBC)
- C-Reactive Protein (CRP)
- Blood Culture
- Liver Function Test (LFT)
- Urinalysis
